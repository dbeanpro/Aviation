eu-LISA

In the very near future (2024-2025), the EU will begin requiring foreign operators to register with their new "Entry / Exit System" EES for passenger screening. The point of this is to switch from physical record keeping of Visa entries & passport stamping to digital record keeping. The people that are actuating this update are known as eu-LISA (The European Union Agency for the Operational Management of Large Scale IT Systems in the Area of Freedom, Security, and Justice). They manage customs info for travelers entering / exiting the Schengen Area. According to this article, PRIVATE FLIGHTS with non-fee paying passengers on board do not fall under the scope of EES / ETIAS.

ETIAS - European Travel Info and Authorization System

Similar to US ESTA's, this is a visa waiver program for the EU. Basically there are 60ish countries whose citizents are allowed to use ETIAS for Visa waivering.

CPDLC

Controller Pilot Data Link Communications (CPDLC) is a technology that allows pilots and air traffic control to communicate using text-messages. It's a key part of the future air navigation system (FANS), which aims to improve aviation safety and reduce errors by providing a common language that's not affected by accents and/or language barriers.
